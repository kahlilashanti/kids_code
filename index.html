Meet Richard Pryor

a solo show by Kahlil Ashanti

Show Description

The Show

'Hope I'm Funny!'

Based on true events, this one hour interactive theatrical experience takes audiences back to 1950s Peoria Illinois to meet the characters who influenced Richard Pryor. With no costume changes or props NY Times Critics Pick winner Kahlil Ashanti (Basic Training) explores the contradictions, demons and loves of the greatest comedian of our time.
